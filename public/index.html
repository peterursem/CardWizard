CardWizard opens up a realm of new possibilities in the SolutionShop

Designed from the ground up for the CSP model, CardWizard boasts enhanced capabilities compared to the existing workflow. CardWizard enables associates to easily adhere to SolutionShop best practices and provide a smoother experience in store. Empowering our great SolutionShop associates we can help customers to feel more confident in their orders.

CONNECT

CardWizard opens many possibilities for SolutionShops across the country. The platform allows stores to offer many more 1-Hour Services, including:

  • Business Cards
  • Post Cards
  • Greeting Cards
  • Tent Cards
  • Photos

SHARE

With substantially reduced production times CardWizard enables Active Sampling in store.

Customers can try out different card designs and paper types before committing to a larger batch, and associates are better able to recommend products.

PARTNER

Errors can be corrected immediately using CardWizard. Improperly folded cards, design errors, and quality concerns can be effectively and quickly resolved in-store rather than waiting for another production run from the hub.

This helps stores keep up the Big Print Promise and deliver cards done right and on time.

While our effect on customer's experience is profound, it doesn't end there. CardWizard packs a punch under the hood, and has a number of distinct advantages over the tools currently in use.

Security at it's Core.

We all need to be diligent to mitigate cyber attacks. The more we send over the network the more vulnerable our customers' information is. Currently, SolutionBuilder photo orders are uploaded to a central server where they are available publicly without any authentication. CardWizard keeps customer info secure offline.

It all makes cents.

By processing information on the computers already installed in SolutionShop, server costs are greatly reduced. The only hosting required for CardWizard are static assets which can be cached internally, provided at a low cost from any basic web server, or installed right on SolutionShop PCs.

Bullet time.

One of the biggest problems plagueing SolutionShops is slow network speeds. Uploading files to our print server is a major bottleneck in the ordering process. By reducing preflight time CardWizard makes these products available for 1-Hour production windows.
